Is Patholytica free to use?

Yes! All content and tools are free for students worldwide.

Can I contribute content?

Absolutely! Students can submit notes, quizzes, and tutorials to the platform.

Do I need an account?

You can browse most resources freely, but creating an account allows tracking your progress and participating in the community.
