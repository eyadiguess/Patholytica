Frequently Asked Questions

Is Patholytica free to use?

Yes! All content and tools are free for students worldwide. We believe quality medical education should be accessible to everyone.

Can I contribute content?

Absolutely! Students can submit notes, quizzes, and tutorials to the platform. We encourage community contributions to help fellow learners.

Do I need an account?

You can browse most resources freely, but creating an account allows tracking your progress and participating in the community.

What medical topics are covered?

We cover all major medical disciplines including anatomy, physiology, pathology, pharmacology, and clinical sciences with materials suitable for all levels.

How often is content updated?

Our community regularly contributes new content and updates existing materials to ensure everything remains current and accurate.
